Balloon Archery

An physics-inspired archery challenge. Release arrows, clear balloons, and climb the ranks.

How to Play:

  • Rotate your bow organically by shifting your mouse pointer.
  • Left-click / Tap the screen to shoot an arrow along that calculated path.
  • Vibrant balloons will float upwards. Burst them to level up!
  • Avoid missing entirely or letting balloons float off the screen—both will deduct 1 life!
